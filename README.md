 What I did?
I took a huge UFC dataset (6,500+ rows) and turned it into an interactive dashboard. Here is the simple breakdown:

I created a "Surprise" metric: I didn't just look at who won. I used a formula to see if the "underdog" beat the "favorite" based on betting odds.

I cleaned and organized the data: I managed 6,529 rows and dozens of columns using Pivot Tables to make sure everything was accurate.

I made it interactive: I added "Slicers." You can click on a country or gender, and the whole chart updates instantly.
